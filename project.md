# Albion Online Guild Management Platform

## Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive web platform designed for the Albion Online community. The website serves as a centralized hub for Guild management, Alliance coordination, Marketplace activities, Character Builds, and various game-related tools.

The goal is to provide players, guild leaders, officers, and alliance members with an all-in-one solution to manage their activities efficiently and improve collaboration within the Albion Online ecosystem.

---

## Objectives

- Simplify Guild and Alliance management.
- Streamline guild recruitment and application processing.
- Provide a dedicated Marketplace for player trading.
- Enable players to create, share, and discover character builds.
- Offer useful calculators for crafting, economy, fame, and resource management.
- Improve communication, organization, and member engagement.
